Mind-meld to make better decisions

Gain confidence in your software stack selection with crowd-sourced and AI insights flagging concerns and also recommendations on how to address them.

Software Dependencies

Developers often ask themselves these questions:

  • Is this the right dependency to add for the feature I need to build?
  • What open source libraries and/or packages are others using?
  • I need a stable and secure version. Does it conform to company policies?

These are important questions that a developer needs to answer every day, but choosing and managing the software stack doesn’t add any value to the application. Today’s software developers are challenged to write applications with an increased speed and agility. These challenges are often met by choosing new open source libraries that are rapidly evolving and it’s a challenge to keep up with the various open source projects and potential alternatives. In addition, enterprise developers need to meet a certain set of company standards and general compliance policies.

Information and recommendations in itself are rarely useful. In an agile development world information needs to be distilled immediately and appropriate actions taken. In a team, collaboration and information exchange are paramount.

OpenShift.io improves information and communication

Full stack assessment

Openshift.io analytics provides full application stack assessment.

Providing developers with important information and analysis on your application dependencies concerning licenses, security, and software quality is a fundamental capability. Data is presented on different levels of granularity in an easily consumable and accessible format. Most relevant information for fast assessment are highlighted.

Actionable insights

Insights and recommendations are fully integrated into the developer workflow.

Only actionable information is valuable. This is why we integrate deeply in different areas of the development workflow. We present information when changes are made and alternative options can easily be evaluated. Closing the feedback cycle as early as possible, we present information during development in the IDE. A full stack review is presented during each fully automated build. In each phase, the developer can plan and act upon the recommendations and get help from team members.

Deep Analytics

Deep analytics to give you insights beyond plain static analysis.

We go beyond highlighting simple concerns. We provide the full picture of your application stack in the relevant context. We perform a continuous analysis of crowd-sourced data and give recommendations that are relevant to your concerns. As we learn together, our system acquires more insights that are specific to your and your team's concerns.
